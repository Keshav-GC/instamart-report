Brand Insights Aggregator

This tool will process your Brand Insights CSV file to:

  1. Drop City, Product Dimension (PRODUCT_NAME), L1_CATEGORY, and L2_CATEGORY.
  2. Aggregate the data by all other descriptive columns.
  3. Recalculate TOTAL_CTR, A2C_RATE, and TOTAL_ROI.

Please select your CSV file.
